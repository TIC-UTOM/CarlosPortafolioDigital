SUMMER 2019 ART DIRECTION. EDITORIAL. DIGITAL.
Pique-Nique Cafe is a (hypothetical) breakfast and lunch cafe. Having a picnic theme, this cafe specializes in "make your own" picnic to-go orders. A café is a type of restaurant which typically serves coffee and tea, in addition to light refreshments such as baked goods or snacks. The term "café" comes from the French word meaning "coffee". This branding project consisted of a logo, menu, postcard, poster, business card and packaging design. Bright and inviting colors were used to best suite the young and adventurous demographic of Portland, OR.
